- [Ten simple rules for structuring papers](https://journals.plos.org/ploscompbiol/article?id=10.1371/journal.pcbi.1005619)
- [How to write a first-class paper](https://www.nature.com/articles/d41586-018-02404-4)

How to make good figures and figure legends:
- [Creating clear and informative image-based figures for scientific publications](https://journals.plos.org/plosbiology/article?id=10.1371/journal.pbio.3001161)
